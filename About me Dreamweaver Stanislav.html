My IT Journey at CAVC

Hi! I am currently studying for the Extended Certificate in IT (Level 2) at Cardiff and Vale College. This course is practical and work-related, which means we learn by completing realistic assignments and projects.

I chose this program because I want to understand how computer systems work and develop technical skills for my future career. The facilities and IT labs at the college are great, and they help us get real hands-on experience every day.

My goal is to successfully complete this Level 2 qualification, progress to Level 3 Computing next year, and eventually build a professional career in the technology sector.
