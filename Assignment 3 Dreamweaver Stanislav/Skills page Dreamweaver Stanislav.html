What skills I will get from this course?   

The Extended Certificate in IT covers a variety of subject areas. These modules give us a solid foundation and a good knowledge of different computing career paths:

• Technology Systems & The Online World: Learning the core fundamentals of how computer systems, hardware, networks, and internet technologies interact.

• Website & Software Development: Developing practical programming skills, writing clean code, and building interactive web platforms.

• Multimedia & Digital Portfolio: Creating digital content and building a professional portfolio to showcase all the projects and assignments we complete during the course.
