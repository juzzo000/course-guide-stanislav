Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Do I need to know how to code before starting?

A: No! This Level 2 course is perfect for beginners. The teachers explain everything from the basics of HTML, CSS, and data logic step by step.

Q: How are we assessed on this course?

A: Most modules are assessed through practical coursework assignments. However, units like Technology Systems and The Online World have external exams.

Q: Can I progress to Level 3 after completing this?

A: Yes! Successfully passing this course with good grades allows you to progress the IT Level 3 Extended Diploma at CAVC.
